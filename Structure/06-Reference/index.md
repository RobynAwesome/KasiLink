# KasiLink Information Library

> Source-backed content for the app, including FAQs, services, government material, and issue context.

## What Lives Here

- Government PDFs and source documents.
- FAQ content for users.
- Service directories and issue references.
- Archive indexes that explain what each source is for.

## Best Fit

- `app/*` pages that need factual content.
- community trust and resource pages.
- support content that should be source-backed instead of invented.

## Color Filters

- <span style="color:#dc2626">Critical</span> - source content that the app should not misstate.
- <span style="color:#ea580c">High</span> - user-facing guidance or factual support material.
- <span style="color:#ca8a04">Medium</span> - reference content for future features.
- <span style="color:#16a34a">Low</span> - supporting or background information.

## Extra Filters

- `Source-backed` - verified against official documents.
- `FAQ` - user questions and answers.
- `Service` - government or community services.
- `Issue` - known community pain point or platform problem area.
- `Archive` - PDFs and source indexes.

## Subfolder Guides

- [About Government/](About%20Government/) - government source PDFs.
- [About SA/](About%20SA/) - SA statistics and context notes.
- [Blogs/](Blogs/) - government blog/PDF source material.
- [FAQ/](FAQ/) - user question content.
- [Issues/](Issues/) - issue and pain-point notes.
- [Service/](Service/) - service directory content.
