# Youth Employment And Skills Rationale
> Sources: `youth-employment.md`, `world-Youth-Skills-Day_Empowering-Youth-Through-Skills-Development.pdf`
> Use in app: `/tutoring`, homepage framing, recruiter/judge narrative

## Direct facts

- Government allocated R800 million in 2025 through the National Skills Fund for digital and technology skills.
- PYEI workplace experience reached 156,587 young people between April 2020 and December 2024.
- Higher Health targets 250,000 young people in the next two years.

## Product interpretation

- Tutoring should be framed as part of a skills-to-income pathway, not an isolated education feature.
- KasiLink can credibly position itself as a bridge between immediate earning and longer-term skills participation.
