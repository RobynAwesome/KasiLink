## What This Means for KasiLink

- The app operates in a country actively investing in job creation at scale (R7.7bn EPWP alone)
- 26.5 million grant recipients represent a large informal/gig economy base
- 11+ million indigent households are the target users — proximity and low-data access matter
- Recovery is real but gradual — township residents need tools that meet them where they are now
- KasiLink complements government employment programmes — it is not competing with them
