# KasiLink Blueprint

KasiLink is a township-first, mobile-first Progressive Web App for instant local gigs, trusted service discovery, and community coordination in Gauteng.

## Core Mission

- Connect local talent to nearby work fast.
- Reduce transport and trust friction for township users.
- Keep the product usable on low data, slow devices, and during utility disruption.
- Celebrate community hustle, not just transactions.
