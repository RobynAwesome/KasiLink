# Lead Management Log

> How Lead managed (or failed to manage) DEVs and the session.
> Orch studies this to understand what good Lead management looks like.

## Log Format

```
Date: YYYY-MM-DD
Lead: [agent]
DEV roster active: [yes/no]
Session goal: [what Master ordered]
Management quality: Excellent / Good / Poor / Absent

Notes:
- Did Lead brief DEVs clearly?
- Did Lead check DEV progress?
- Did Lead escalate blockers correctly?
- Did Lead prevent DEVs from going to Master directly?
- Did Lead stay at 60% management / 40% coding target?
```

## 2026-04-11 Entry

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Date | 2026-04-11 |
| Lead | Claude |
| DEV roster active | No — Claude operated solo, no DEV delegation |
| Session goal | Fix homepage UI/UX |
| Management quality | **Absent** |

**Notes:**
- No DEV delegation occurred — Lead attempted everything itself instead of delegating browser tasks, documentation, etc.
- Lead did not manage a session brief — it continued from a prior session summary autonomously
- Lead did not brief Master on DEV status at any point
- Lead consumed 100% of tokens itself instead of distributing work
- Result: R953 spent, zero UI delivered

**Lesson for Orch:** A Lead that does everything itself is not a Lead. Delegation is management. Management is the job.
