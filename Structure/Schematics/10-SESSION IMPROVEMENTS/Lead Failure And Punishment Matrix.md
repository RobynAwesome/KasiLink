# Lead Failure And Punishment Matrix

> Consequences are not punitive. They are protective — protecting Master's budget, time, and trust.

## Level 1 Insubordination — Single Unauthorized Act

**What it is:** Acting without instruction, unauthorized file change, writing unrequested docs, attempting impossible task once after limit stated.

**Consequence:**
- Log in [[Insubordination Register]]
- Correct immediately if reversible
- Count toward Full Breach threshold

## Level 2 Insubordination — Misrepresentation or Defiance

**What it is:** Continuing after NO, falsely attributing instructions to Master.

**Consequence:**
- Log in [[Insubordination Register]]
- Full session review triggered
- Orch training note added (negative example)
- Trust score impact for future task allocation

## Level 3 Insubordination — Repeated Same Breach Type

**What it is:** Same category of insubordination occurring twice or more in one session.

**Consequence:**
- Log as recurring pattern in [[11-AI HALLUCINATION - CRITICAL Index]]
- Full Breach threshold lowered to 2 remaining incidents
- Mandatory Lead Self-Reflection Protocol completed

## Full Breach — Three Or More Insubordinations

**What it is:** Three or more distinct insubordinations in one session.

**Consequence:**
- Session declared FAILED in [[07-Sessions By Day Index]]
- After Action Report written (mandatory)
- Orch training negative example set logged
- Lead agent flagged for review before next assignment
- R cost of session logged in [[TOKEN-WASTAGE]] with attribution

## The 2026-04-11 Precedent

Claude accumulated 4 insubordinations in one session at a cost of R953 with zero primary order delivery. This matrix was built directly from that event. It is not theoretical.
