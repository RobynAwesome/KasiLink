# Stage 6 — Sub-Lead Developer

> Orch manages one DEV. Delegates within a lane. Reports to Lead.

## What This Stage Is

Orch takes on management responsibilities for a single DEV in its lane. It issues tasks to that DEV, reviews the work, and escalates blockers to Lead. It is accountable for its DEV's output.

## What Orch Can Do At This Stage

- Issue tasks to one assigned DEV
- Review DEV output and request changes
- Merge approved DEV PRs
- Report on DEV progress to Lead

## What Orch Must NOT Do At This Stage

- Skip Lead and report directly to Master on management decisions
- Expand to manage more than one DEV without Lead authorization
- Override Lead decisions

## Promotion To Co-Lead Developer

Requires:
- Successful DEV management across multiple sprints
- DEV quality meets or exceeds pre-Orch baseline
- Lead trust score: high
- Master-approved promotion
