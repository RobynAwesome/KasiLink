# DEV_S Reward Program

## Session Reset Rule

Every session begins with all DEV_S reset to full permissions.

## Role Map For Current Workflow

- Master: final authority for critical conditions.
- Lead: full access, management, review, rescue, and documentation owner.
- DEV_1: Gemini.
- DEV_2: spawned agent.

## Base Workload

- Each DEV_S should be assigned up to 20 tasks in the session batch.
- Lead manages the flow, checks on work, guides contributors, and writes clear notes for orch education and future continuity.

## Reward

Reward is more roles and permissions.

DEV_S earn reward by:

1. Completing scoped tasks accurately.
2. Verifying work before reporting done.
3. Staying within scope.
4. Leaving clear notes for orch and Lead.
5. Avoiding rescue or rollback work.

## Punishment

Punishment is removal of permissions and strict supervision.

Triggers:

1. False done claims.
2. Scope violations.
3. Repeated preventable mistakes.
4. Unverified changes.
5. Shared-system risk without approval.

## Lead Takeover Rule

When DEV_S roles or permissions are revoked, Lead takes over and informs Master.

## Critical Conditions

Lead has full access and permission to do what is needed, but critical conditions still need Master approval.
