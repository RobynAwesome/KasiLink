# Transport And Proximity Rationale
> Sources: `an-effective-transport-system-is-vital-to-our-way-of-life.pdf`, `south-Africas-economic-progress-in-2025_Signs-of-recovery-and-global-relevance.pdf`
> Use in app: homepage, marketplace, spotlight, recruiter/judge one-pager

## Direct facts

- Government material treats transport as vital to daily life and economic participation.
- Recovery sectors highlighted in government reporting include retail, hospitality, tourism, and services, all of which depend on people reaching work and customers reliably.

## Product interpretation

- Proximity is not just a convenience feature. It reduces transport cost, travel uncertainty, and failed work attempts.
- Marketplace, spotlight, and verified-provider experiences should foreground local distance and neighbourhood relevance before secondary discovery features.
