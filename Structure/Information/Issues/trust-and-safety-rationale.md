# Trust And Safety Rationale
> Sources: `service-directory.md`, `every-person-must-be-able-live-dignity-comfort-and-peace.pdf`, `the-National-Dialogue-must-be-a-place-for-women-to-raise-their-voices.pdf`
> Use in app: `/verified`, `/forum`, `/community-status`, trust/help copy, recruiter/judge framing

## Direct facts

- Government source material consistently ties dignity, safety, and quality of life to service delivery and community stability.
- Local government and ward structures remain key routes for escalating service and safety concerns.

## Product interpretation

- Trust on KasiLink must be shown through verified providers, community reporting, visible response behaviour, and local accountability signals.
- Forum, verified directory, and community status should feel connected parts of one trust system rather than unrelated features.
