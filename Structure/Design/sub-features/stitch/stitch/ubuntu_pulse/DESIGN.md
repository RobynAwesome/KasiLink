# Design System Document

This document outlines the core visual characteristics of our design system.

## Color Palette

Our color palette is built around a vibrant, light theme, ensuring accessibility and brand consistency.

-   **Primary Color:** `#f1e259` - This bright, energetic yellow is our primary brand color, used for key interactive elements and calls to action.
-   **Secondary Color:** `#d1a10a` - A deeper, rich yellow that complements our primary color, suitable for less prominent UI elements and secondary actions.
-   **Tertiary Color:** `#adac00` - A muted, olive-greenish yellow that serves as an additional accent, perfect for highlights or decorative elements.
-   **Neutral Color:** `#807040` - A warm, earthy brown that provides a solid foundation for backgrounds, surfaces, and non-chromatic elements.

## Typography

We employ a balanced typographic system to ensure readability and hierarchy.

-   **Headlines:** `publicSans` - Used for all major headings and titles, providing a clean and modern feel.
-   **Body Text:** `publicSans` - Our choice for all long-form and general body text, ensuring legibility across various screen sizes.
-   **Labels:** `notoSerif` - A distinctive serif font for labels and specific UI components, offering a touch of classic elegance.

## Shape and Spacing

These properties define the physical appearance and arrangement of our UI components.

-   **Roundedness:** Our components feature a moderate roundedness, striking a balance between modern aesthetics and approachability. This ensures a friendly yet professional look.
-   **Spacing:** We maintain a normal level of whitespace throughout the UI, providing adequate breathing room between elements without feeling sparse or overly dense. This standard spacing promotes clarity and ease of use.
