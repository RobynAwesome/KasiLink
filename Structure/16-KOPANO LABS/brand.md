# Kopano Labs Brand

---

## Name

**Kopano** — from Sesotho and Setswana, meaning *unity, convergence, coming together.*
It is not a tech buzzword. It is a real word with real meaning in the communities we serve.

**Kopano Labs** — the company.
**Kopano Context (KC)** — the intelligence layer.
**Kopano Studio** — the internal orchestration control plane.

---

## Voice

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Direct, grounded, clear | Corporate, jargon-heavy |
| Acknowledge real conditions (load-shedding, unemployment) | Pretend problems don't exist |
| Township-first framing | "Emerging market" framing |
| Honest about limitations | Overclaim AI capabilities |
| Celebrate community wins | Centre the technology over the people |

---

## Design Principles (inherited from KasiLink design system)

| Token | Value | Purpose |
|-------|-------|---------|
| `--color-primary` | `#4595C0` | Brand blue — trust, water, sky |
| `--color-success` | green | Community wins, verification |
| `--color-danger` | red | Urgency, alerts |
| Font — headline | `font-black`, tight tracking | Authority, confidence |
| Font — body | `text-sm leading-7` | Readability at small sizes |
| Radius | `rounded-2xl` / `rounded-xl` | Approachable, not corporate |
