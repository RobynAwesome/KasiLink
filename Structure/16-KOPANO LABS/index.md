> **Kopano Labs builds technology for the township economy —
> where 57% of youth are unemployed, infrastructure is unreliable,
> and proximity is the most undervalued resource.**

We do not build for the "emerging market" as an afterthought.
We build *from* the township, *for* the township, with full product craft.

---

## The Labs Thesis

Three things are true about township South Africa that most tech ignores:

1. **Proximity is economic** — Transport cost is a real barrier. Nearby work is categorically different from far work.
2. **Trust is the product** — In the absence of formal credentials, community reputation is the only signal that works.
3. **Infrastructure is variable** — Load-shedding, water outages, and connectivity gaps are not edge cases. They are the baseline.

Every Kopano Labs product is built around these three truths.

## Labs Principles

1. **Build for the ground truth** — what township residents actually experience
2. **Earn trust before claiming intelligence** — KC proves itself task by task
3. **Schematics is the brain** — all decisions are documented before they are coded
4. **No phantom completions** — in Labs culture, honest failure beats dishonest success
5. **Token discipline is product discipline** — efficiency in AI = efficiency in product
