# 08-IDEAS AT BIRTH Index

> Incubation-only idea bank for KasiLink.
> Ideas live here until they pass a research gate or validation step.
> No idea leaves this folder without a clear implementation option and next action.

## Standing Note

- ideas enter here at birth — raw, unvalidated, not yet scoped
- an idea in this folder is NOT a commitment to build
- every idea needs: signal source, core concept, implementation options, competitor comparison, next action
- ideas move out of this folder when Master approves them for scoping
- ideas are killed here if they fail the research gate — log the kill reason

## Idea Entry Protocol

Use the [[Idea Template]] from `Templates/` when adding a new idea.

Required fields:
1. **Signal source** — where did the idea come from?
2. **Core concept** — one paragraph
3. **Implementation options** — at least 2
4. **Competitor comparison** — who else does this and how are we different?
5. **Next action** — what research gate or validation step must pass?
