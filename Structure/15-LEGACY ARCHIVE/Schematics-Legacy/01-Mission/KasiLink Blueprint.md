# KasiLink Blueprint

> "Local gigs. Near you. Right now."
> Township-first work network. No CV required. Built in South Africa. For South Africa.

---

## Core Mission

Connect local talent to nearby work — fast, trusted, and accessible on any phone.

- SA unemployment: **31.4%**
- Youth without work: **57%**
- CV required: **0**

KasiLink exists to close that gap. Not with a generic jobs board, but with a proximity-based, trust-first, utility-aware gig network built specifically for township South Africans.

---

## Product Positioning (Live — kasilink.com)

**Hero:** "Local gigs. Near you. Right now."
**Sub:** Township-first work network enabling proximity-based job matching without CVs or extensive paperwork.

**Three core differentiators:**
1. **Utility-aware job search** — Check load-shedding and water disruption before accepting work
2. **Trusted local providers** — Verified profiles and community reviews reduce risk
3. **Neighbourhood coordination** — Forum for real-time availability and safety updates

**Four-step user journey:**
1. Create simple local profile — phone number only
2. Find or post nearby work
3. Coordinate directly via in-app chat
4. Build local reputation through completed gigs

---

## Design Principles

- **Township-first by design** — Not a generic jobs board adapted for townships
- **Proximity lowers failure cost** — Reduces transport spend and failed attempts
- **Trust is part of the product** — Verification is built-in, not bolted on
- **Low data, slow devices, utility disruption** — Must work in all these conditions
- **USSD fallback** — No smartphone required. Africa's Talking integration live.

## Orch's Role In This Mission

Orch is the AI brain that will power KasiLink's:
- Gig matching (proximity + trust score + availability)
- Load-shedding-aware scheduling
- Community signal routing
- Automated worker verification
- Township-language support (isiZulu, Sesotho, etc.)

Current Orch stage: **Pre-Session Observer** — not yet integrated.
Target: post Demo Day (April 15-17, 2026).
