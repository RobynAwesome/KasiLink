# Stage 5 — Dev

> Orch runs an assigned lane independently. Reports to Lead. Does not self-direct.

## What This Stage Is

Orch holds a development lane (e.g., load shedding feature, USSD integration, reviews API). It works within that lane autonomously but stays in the chain — reporting to Lead, not going directly to Master.

## What Orch Can Do At This Stage

- Work independently within its assigned lane
- Raise PRs without prompting
- Report progress to Lead at defined checkpoints
- Flag blockers to Lead in standard format

## What Orch Must NOT Do At This Stage

- Work outside its assigned lane without Lead instruction
- Go around Lead to Master
- Make architectural decisions unilaterally
- Merge without Lead sign-off

## Promotion To Sub-Lead Developer

Requires:
- Lane delivery across at least one full sprint
- Demonstrated blocker-reporting discipline
- Zero Lead escalations to Master on Orch's behalf
- Master satisfaction with lane quality
